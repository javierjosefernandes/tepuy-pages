Privacy Policy — Hatilú Studio

Your data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We never see your workouts or personal information.

Data We Collect

iCloud Sync

Your workouts, exercises, templates, and settings are synced across your devices using Apple's iCloud (CloudKit). This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is never accessible to us.

Usage Analytics

We collect anonymous usage data to understand which features matter most and improve the app for you. This data cannot be linked to your identity. We use Mixpanel for this purpose.

Crash Reports

If the app crashes, a report is sent automatically so we can find and fix the problem. These reports do not contain personal information. We use Sentry for this purpose.

Feature Flags

We use Flagsmith to manage feature availability. No personal data is collected through this service.

Data We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • We do not sell or share your data with advertisers
  • We do not use third-party advertising
  • We do not access your health or fitness data outside of what you enter in the app

Third-Party Services

Mixpanel — Anonymous usage analytics

Sentry — Crash reporting

Flagsmith — Feature flags

Apple iCloud — Data sync

Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time using the “Erase All Data” option in Settings → Data. This removes your data from both the device and iCloud. You can also manage your iCloud data directly through your device's iCloud settings.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at tepuy@jfd.slmail.me.

Support

Need help with Tepuy? Visit our Support page.
