Tepuy

Roadmap — Hatilú Studio

Built for athletes who take their training seriously. Here’s what Tepuy can do for you today — and what’s coming next.

What You Can Do Today

Everything you need to design, track, and understand your training — free and without limits.

Design Your Training

Create custom exercises for any discipline. Build workout templates that match exactly how you train — your movements, your structure, your way.

Track Every Session

Log workouts from a template or go freestyle. Record sets, reps, weight, and more. Forgot to log a session? Add it after the fact.

See Your Progress

Charts that show how you’re improving over time. Personal records tracked automatically. Progression insights for every exercise and workout.

Your Dashboard

Today’s snapshot, quick actions, recent activity, and your latest PRs — all in one place.

Works Everywhere

Optimized for iPhone and iPad. Syncs seamlessly across your devices with iCloud. Available in 8 languages.

Coming Next

These features are on the way — and they’ll be free, just like everything above.

Scheduling

Plan your training week. Set recurring workouts. Get reminders so you never miss a session.

Apple Ecosystem

Apple Health, Widgets, Live Activities, Apple Watch, and Apple Music — your training, deeply connected to the ecosystem you already use.

Goals & Streaks

Set targets, track consistency, and build momentum with streaks that keep you accountable.

Sharing

Export workouts as PDF. Import and export your training data — because your data is yours.

Social

Share to Strava and social media. Let others see your progress and celebrate your wins.

Premium Companions

Workout tracking is — and will always be — completely free. That’s the foundation of Tepuy, and we’re not putting it behind a paywall. Premium adds optional companion tools that go beyond training logs: deeper insights, personalized guidance, and wellness tracking. They’re here to complement your training, never to limit it.

AI Coaching

Your personal coach, available anytime. Get tailored advice on workouts, nutrition, cooking, and recovery — all informed by your actual training data.

Body Composition

Track weight, body fat, measurements, and see your transformation over time.

Nutrition & Supplements

Log meals, macros, supplements, and hydration. Set reminders and track adherence.

Recovery

Monitor sleep quality, readiness, fatigue, and keep a pain and injury log.

Advanced Insights

Post-workout analytics, long-term trends, cross-domain correlations, and smart recommendations to take your training further.
